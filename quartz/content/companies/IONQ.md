# IONQ — IonQ, Inc.

**Sector:** Technology | **Exchange:** NYSE | **Updated:** 2026-04-08

IonQ is a quantum computing company developing trapped-ion quantum computers. First pure-play quantum computing company to go public (2021). Expanding into quantum networking, sensing, and security.

## Analysis Notes

### FY2025 vs FY2024 — Narrative Evolution Analysis *(2026-04-03)*

**Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐

**Tags:** `strategy`

### Narrative Drift
IonQ evolved from quantum computing company to 'quantum platform company' across computing, networking, sensing and security. Losses increased substantially ($510.4M vs $331.6M).
### Red Flags
- Algorithmic Qubit metric no longer emphasized
- Net losses +54% YoY
- Cash 9x growth likely from acquisitions/financing, not operational improvement
- New satellite imagery revenue stream may boost top-line without core business improvement

### FY2025 10-K Analysis *(2026-04-03)*

**Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐

**Tags:** `strategy` `growth`

### Financial Performance
IonQ in high-growth, pre-profitability phase. Revenue FY2025: $130M (+202% YoY). Net loss: $510.4M. Cash & equivalents grew dramatically from $363.8M to $3,336.8M. Acquisitions: Oxford Ionics, Lightsynq Technologies.
### Strategic Priorities
- Trapped-ion architecture with clearest path to fault-tolerant QC
- Acquisitions to accelerate roadmap (Electronic Qubit Control, photonic interconnects)
- Expand quantum ecosystem (networking, sensing, security)
- Pending SkyWater Technology acquisition for secure quantum foundry
### Management Tone
Bullish
