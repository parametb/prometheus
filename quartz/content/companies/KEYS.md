Keysight FY2025 revenue $5.38B (+8.0% recovery from $4.98B trough). Net income $0.85B. FY2024 weakness from telecom/semiconductor inventory correction. FY2025 recovery powered by Spirent, Synopsys OSG, and Ansys PowerArtist acquisitions. Backlog grew to $2.7B from $2.4B.
