### FY2025 Annual Report Analysis
★★☆☆☆
*#strategy #valuation*

[Financial Performance]
Revenue fell 17% to £2.46B. Operating income negative (-£3M) vs £418M in FY2024. Net loss -£75M. Gross margin 62.5%. FCF held at £278M. Dividend suspended for FY2025.
[Strategic Focus — Burberry Forward]
New CEO Joshua Schulman launched 'Burberry Forward': 1) Elevate outerwear as primary brand identity 2) Reduce wholesale channel 3) £100M annualised cost savings by FY27 4) Streamline SKU count 5) Rebuild brand desirability.
H2 comparable retail sales -5% vs H1 -20% — trajectory improving.
[Management Tone]
Cautiously optimistic
